**📡 Wi-Fi Training Program**

**📖 Overview**
This repository/document contains the complete work and learning outcomes from the Wi-Fi Training Program assigned during the third month of training. The program is designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure to wireless networking concepts, configurations, and troubleshooting techniques.


**🎯 Training Objectives**

The key objectives of this training program are:

Understand core Wi-Fi technologies and standards
Learn how wireless networks operate within the OSI model
Gain hands-on experience with Wi-Fi configuration and optimization
Analyze different Wi-Fi bands, protocols, and performance factors
Understand network design, topologies, and deployment strategies
Develop basic troubleshooting and diagnostic skills


**🛠️ Practical Activities**

Throughout the training program, the following hands-on tasks were performed:

Configuring Wi-Fi networks on different frequency bands
Observing and analyzing device capabilities
Comparing network performance under different conditions
Implementing different Wi-Fi modes (Bridge, Repeater, etc.)
Basic troubleshooting and performance testing


**📊 Key Learnings**

Wi-Fi operates across Physical and Data Link layers
Performance depends on frequency, interference, and environment
Proper network design improves scalability and user experience
Security is critical in wireless communication
Practical configuration enhances understanding of theoretical concepts


**🚀 Conclusion**

The Wi-Fi Training Program provides a comprehensive understanding of wireless networking, combining theoretical concepts with practical implementation. This training serves as a strong foundation for working with real-world wireless systems and advanced networking technologies.
